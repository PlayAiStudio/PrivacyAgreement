In certainly specific cases, we use the  
android.permission.QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission in our app to improve the  
advertising experience for our users. Our advertising partners utilize this permission

overall user experience. It's important to note that this permission doesn't allow us or  
our partners to access personal or sensitive information from other applications, it  
merely helps in identifying the presence of an app.
